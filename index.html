Akan Names

Akan (Twi, Asante) names are the names used by the Akan people of Ghana and the Ivory Coast, who are frequently naming their baby boys and baby girls after the day of the week in which they were born and the order of birth for each of them. More on Akan names can be found on Wikipedia
